Ramen

Home

Description

Ramen is a popular dish, especially in Japan.

This recipe is only about the soup and noodles, though ramen often contains other ingredients, such as eggs or meat.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups vegetable broth
  • 500 g ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 3 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili oil
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 2 green onions

Steps

  1. Combine broth and noodle and bring to a boil over high heat
  2. Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chili oil, and ginger.
  3. Add sesame oil
    4. +
  4. Add green onions on top and serve.
