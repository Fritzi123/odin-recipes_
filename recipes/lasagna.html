Description

TheLasagna is a traditional Italian dish. It is very common in Italy and tastes very good.

It is also famous all over the world, so there are many different recipes for it.

Ingredients

  • Lean ground beef
  • One egg
  • One onion and two gloves of garlic
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
